# SEO Competitor Analysis — Ghost Portfolio
**Date:** 2026-03-15
**Analyst:** SEO Specialist Agent
**Scope:** Dental tourism (veneers, implants) + hair transplant medical tourism targeting UK/EU patients considering Albania

---

## Summary

Ten competitor sites analysed across the three niches. Key takeaways:

1. **Dental Departures dominates Albania-specific dental search** with a 2,500+ word location guide, price-comparison table, and clinic directory.
2. **Content depth is the #1 differentiator** — every high-ranking site has 1,000–3,000+ words per page and a blog/guide library.
3. **Schema adoption is inconsistent** — even major players skip JSON-LD, creating an opportunity for our sites to stand out in rich results.
4. **Price transparency wins clicks** — all top performers publish exact comparison tables (UK price vs. destination price).
5. **Hreflang and multilingual** is a growth lever only the biggest players use — not yet needed at our stage.

## SEO Strategy Recommendations

### What Competitors Do That We Should Copy

1. **Price comparison tables** — Dental Departures shows exact UK vs Albania pricing inline on the page. Do the same on all three sites. Consider adding a visible comparison table to each landing page (not just text).

2. **FAQ section with schema** — Add `FAQPage` JSON-LD schema to all three sites. Treatment Abroad and others use FAQ content to capture featured snippet positions.

3. **Word count** — Dental Departures Albania guide is 2,500+ words. Our pages are ~800–1,000 words of visible content. Expand each site by adding:
   - "Why Albania" long-form section (already partially present)
   - Travel + logistics section (flight times, Tirana basics)
   - Detailed "what to expect" step-by-step

4. **Patient journey content** — Competitors who rank well explain the full journey: consultation → travel → procedure → aftercare. Our sites do this but briefly. Expand the "how it works" to 3–5 steps with more detail.

5. **Blog / article content** — All major players have blog/guide libraries. Without blog content, we can't rank for long-tail informational queries. Recommend: 2–4 blog posts per site targeting informational queries ("Is Albania safe for dental work?", "FUE vs DHI: which is right for me?", "Albania vs Turkey for hair transplants").

### What Competitors Miss That We Already Do Better

| We have | Competitors often lack |
|---|---|
| JSON-LD structured data with price offers | Most competitor landing pages have no JSON-LD |
| Twitter Card meta tags | Most competitors missing Twitter Card |
| WhatsApp-first conversion flow | Standard form-only UX |
| Specific "starting from" pricing in H1 | Generic titles without price hooks |
| Single-niche domain focus (topical relevance) | Broad directories with diluted niche authority |

### Schema Gaps to Close

All three sites still missing:

- **`FAQPage` JSON-LD** — add to capture FAQ rich results for "are Albanian dentists qualified?", "how much do veneers cost in Albania?" etc.
- **`BreadcrumbList`** — single-page sites don't need this now, but will need it if blog is added
- **`LocalBusiness` `openingHours`** — currently no hours set in schema (fair since it's a lead-gen site, not a clinic)
