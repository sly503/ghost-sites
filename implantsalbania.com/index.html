Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before your trip.

Do I need an X-ray before contacting you?

No, but if you already have a panoramic X-ray or CT scan, sending it via WhatsApp will help us provide a more accurate quote faster. If you do not have one, we will arrange a free X-ray at the clinic in Tirana.

What implant brands do you use?

Our clinics use Nobel Biocare, Straumann, and other premium European implant systems. These are the same brands used in top clinics across Western Europe, with full manufacturer warranties.

How long do I need to stay in Albania?

Most treatments require 3–5 days in Tirana. For complex cases like All-on-4 or bone grafting, a second visit of 2–3 days may be needed after 3–6 months for the final restoration.

How quickly will I get a response?

WhatsApp enquiries with X-rays typically receive a personalised quote within 24 hours. Form submissions are reviewed and responded to within one business day.
