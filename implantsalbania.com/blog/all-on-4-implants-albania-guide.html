All-on-4 Dental Implants in Albania: The Complete Guide

Published 15 March 2026 • 7 min read

All-on-4 is one of the most significant advances in modern dentistry. It allows patients who have lost most or all of their teeth to receive a full set of fixed, natural-looking teeth — supported by just four implants per arch. It is a life-changing procedure, but in the UK it comes with a life-changing price tag: £10,000 to £15,000 per arch.

In Albania, the same procedure — using the same implant brands and techniques — starts from €3,500 per arch. This guide covers everything you need to know about getting All-on-4 dental implants in Albania: how the procedure works, who it is for, what is included, and how to get started.

What Is All-on-4?

All-on-4 (sometimes written as "All on Four") is a full-arch tooth replacement technique developed by Nobel Biocare. Instead of placing one implant for every missing tooth, four implants are strategically positioned in the jawbone to support an entire arch of 10 to 14 teeth.

The key innovation is the angling of the two rear implants. By tilting them at approximately 30 to 45 degrees, the surgeon can achieve maximum contact with available bone — even in patients who have experienced significant bone loss. This often eliminates the need for bone grafting, which would otherwise add months of healing time and thousands of pounds to the cost.

All-on-4 vs traditional implants: With conventional implants, a full arch might require 6 to 8 individual implants plus bone grafting. All-on-4 achieves a similar result with just four implants and typically no bone graft — meaning fewer surgeries, faster recovery, and significantly lower cost.

Who Is a Good Candidate?

All-on-4 is designed for patients who:

  • Are missing most or all teeth in the upper jaw, lower jaw, or both
  • Have remaining teeth that are severely damaged, decayed, or loose and need extraction
  • Currently wear full dentures and want a fixed, permanent alternative
  • Have been told they do not have enough bone for traditional implants (the angled implant technique often solves this)
  • Want a complete solution in as few visits as possible

Patients must be in generally good health. Uncontrolled diabetes, heavy smoking, and certain medications can affect implant success. Your suitability will be confirmed through a CT scan assessment before any treatment begins.

The All-on-4 Procedure: Step by Step

Here is exactly what happens when you get All-on-4 implants at one of our partner clinics in Tirana.

Before Your Trip

  • Send your records: You send your panoramic X-ray, CT scan, or existing treatment plan via WhatsApp. If you do not have imaging, your local dentist can arrange one, or we will do it at the clinic in Tirana.
  • Receive your treatment plan: Our oral surgery team reviews your case and sends you a detailed treatment plan with transparent pricing within 24 hours.
  • Book your visit: Once you are ready, we help coordinate your flights, hotel near the clinic, and treatment schedule. Most patients arrive the day before their first appointment.

Visit 1: Implant Placement (4–5 days in Tirana)

  • Day 1 — Clinical assessment: A comprehensive in-person examination including 3D CT scan, oral health assessment, and final treatment plan confirmation. Any remaining failing teeth are noted for extraction.
  • Day 2 — Surgery: Under local anaesthesia (or sedation if preferred), any remaining teeth are extracted and the four implants are placed into the jawbone. A temporary fixed prosthetic — your immediate new teeth — is fitted on the same day. You leave the clinic with a full set of functional teeth.
  • Day 3–4 — Recovery and review: You rest at your hotel. A follow-up appointment is scheduled to check healing, adjust your temporary prosthetic if needed, and provide detailed aftercare instructions.
  • Day 5 — Final check and departure: A final review before you fly home. You receive written aftercare guidelines and a direct line to your treatment coordinator for any questions during recovery.

Between Visits: The Healing Period

Over the next 3 to 6 months, your implants undergo osseointegration — the process where the titanium implants fuse with your jawbone. During this time, you wear the temporary fixed prosthetic, which looks and functions like natural teeth. You can eat, speak, and smile normally. Your coordinator checks in regularly to monitor progress.

Visit 2: Final Prosthetic (2–3 days in Tirana)

  • Impressions and fitting: Precise impressions are taken of your healed implants. Your permanent bridge is fabricated — typically from high-grade zirconia or acrylic — and fitted over 2 to 3 days.
  • Final adjustments: The bite, colour, and shape of the final prosthetic are fine-tuned for comfort and a natural appearance. You leave with your permanent teeth.

Recovery and Aftercare

Most patients experience moderate swelling and discomfort for the first 3 to 5 days after implant placement. Pain is managed with prescribed medication. Key aftercare points:

  • Soft food diet for the first 2 to 4 weeks while the implants begin to integrate
  • No smoking during the healing period (smoking significantly increases implant failure risk)
  • Gentle oral hygiene routine as directed by your surgeon
  • Most patients return to work within 3 to 5 days of surgery
  • Full osseointegration takes 3 to 6 months, after which the final prosthetic is fitted
Success rates: All-on-4 implants have a documented success rate of over 95% at the 10-year mark when placed by experienced specialists. Using premium implant brands like Nobel Biocare and maintaining good oral hygiene are the two most important factors for long-term success.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the All-on-4 dental implant procedure?

All-on-4 is a full-arch tooth replacement technique where an entire set of fixed teeth (typically 10–14 teeth per arch) is supported by just four strategically placed dental implants. The two rear implants are tilted at an angle to maximise bone contact, which often eliminates the need for bone grafting.

How much does All-on-4 cost in Albania?

All-on-4 full-arch restoration starts from €3,500 per arch in Albania. This typically includes the four implants, temporary prosthetic, and the final fixed bridge. In the UK, the same procedure costs £10,000–£15,000 per arch.

How long do I need to stay in Albania for All-on-4?

The initial visit typically requires 4–5 days in Tirana for consultation, implant placement, and fitting of the temporary prosthetic. A second visit of 2–3 days is usually needed 3–6 months later for the final fixed bridge to be fitted once the implants have fully integrated with the bone.

Am I a good candidate for All-on-4 implants?

All-on-4 is suitable for patients who are missing most or all teeth in one or both arches, or who have teeth that are failing and need extraction. The tilted implant design means many patients who lack sufficient bone for traditional implants can still be treated without bone grafting. A CT scan assessment will confirm your suitability.

How long do All-on-4 implants last?

The implant fixtures themselves are designed to last a lifetime with proper care. The prosthetic bridge typically lasts 10–20 years before it may need replacing. Regular dental check-ups and good oral hygiene are essential for long-term success.
