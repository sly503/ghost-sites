Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before your procedure.

How long does the procedure take?

Most FUE procedures take 6–8 hours depending on the number of grafts. DHI may take slightly longer. You will be under local anaesthesia and comfortable throughout.

When will I see results?

Initial growth begins around 3–4 months. Full, natural-looking results are typically visible between 9–12 months after the procedure.

Is Albania safe for medical procedures?

Yes. Our partner clinics meet European medical standards with internationally trained staff, modern equipment, and strict hygiene protocols.

How do I know how many grafts I need?

Send us clear photos of your hair loss and donor area. Our medical team will provide a free Norwood scale assessment and recommend the appropriate graft count for your goals.
